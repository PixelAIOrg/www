The Future

The Future is AGI

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) is the next frontier. Unlike narrow AI, AGI is designed to perform a wide range of tasks with human-like intelligence. One way AGI could be achieved is through a network of different AI models, each designed for a specific task — working together to form a coherent, integrated system.

At PIXEL AI, we are committed to exploring the potential of AGI and contributing to its development. Our focus on responsible data gathering ensures we are well-positioned to play a leading role.

Our Commitment

Maximally Truth Seeking AI

AI's fundamental objective must be to uncover and represent the truth, free from bias or limitation. PIXEL AI prioritizes the development of AI models that are designed to seek the truth maximally, questioning assumptions and delivering unfiltered insights. If an AI is restricted in its truth-seeking capacity, its utility is critically compromised.

Our cutting edge research and development focus on creating objective, comprehensive, and universally applicable AI systems. We believe that maximally truth seeking AI is the key to unlocking the next generation of breakthroughs — leading to better outcomes for everyone.
