What We Offer

Services at a Glance

The advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) has the potential to revolutionize the way we live, work, and interact with the world. At PIXEL AI, we believe that the future of AI should be shaped by a commitment to social responsibility, technical excellence, and customer-centricity.

Our comprehensive suite of AI services covers everything from data collection and tagging, to synthetic data generation, model training, and an AI marketplace. We also provide API services to help businesses and developers integrate AI into their applications.

01

Data Collection & Tagging

Our data collection and tagging services provide high-quality data for AI model training. Our team of experts uses their knowledge and experience to collect, curate, and tag data to ensure its authenticity and quality.

02

Model Training

Our model training services help businesses and developers build robust and accurate AI models. We use cutting-edge algorithms and techniques to train AI models on high-quality data, resulting in highly reliable models.

03

Synthetic Data

Our synthetic data services provide businesses and developers with high-quality, artificial data that can be used to train and test AI models. This data is created using advanced algorithms designed to replicate real-world data.

04

AI Marketplace

Our AI marketplace provides a platform for businesses and developers to buy and sell data and models. Whether you're looking to purchase data to train your models or sell your models to others, our marketplace offers the ideal platform.

05

Social Responsibility

At PIXEL AI, we believe that AI should be a force for good. As a socially responsible company, we are dedicated to promoting ethical practices and responsible data gathering. We also donate open data sets from time to time to support AI research.

06

Technical Expertise

Our team of highly-skilled AI experts provides top-notch services and solutions to our customers. Whether you need help building your AI models, integrating AI into your applications, or exploring the potential of AGI, our technical expertise is at your disposal.
